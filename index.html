Safety Through Technology

Lets change the way we think about safety for women and children. Wearable Technology enables empowerment of women where legislation, policy making and the law enforcements have lacked. Women Against Rape is about protecting, preventing and deterring violent crimes against women and children, especially RAPE. There is a huge gap between what is available in the market and the need of the women, these are affordability and versatility. Our Product strategy is not only fill the gaps but drive the convergence between the two through nano-technology enabled sensors, digitally contoured optical lenses for image capturing and leveraging bluetooth for connectivity.


EXECUTING THE VISION

We are taking baby steps by working through one organization in India called DURGA. They are based out of Bangalore, India and making great strides in educating women on how to be in control about their safety. The goal of DURGA is to empower young women to become champions, mentors and facilitators; so that they can be equipped with practical skills that can help them take on everyday harassment bravely. Through interactive theatre games embedded into a 3-hour module that assesses and helps in understanding sexual harassment, an individual’s social environment and his or her response mechanism, Durga will help in distinguishing and responding to challenging situations in a simple, yet effective way. So far, DURGA has successfully touched the lives of nearly 1,000 women across Bangalore, and in the days to come, this number will certainly grow. Our Mission

We want to see SWear button with every single woman on this earth, make it an essential accessory so that she is safe and the people who love her know that she is safe. We are collaborating with organizations like DURGA to help us implement and educate the women about our products. We have also identified school badges as a target product where our product could be attached. We would love to work with Women for Human Rights (WHR) organization to collaborate an implement our product.

Lets Start. W.A.R is about bringing women together to help rape victims around the globe and one day hopefully eradicate rape from our society. Tall order? Perhaps! But if we don't even think about it, how can we achieve any success. Just like we went after Polio eradication and today we successfully can say we do not have Polio in the world, we have to take Rape as a disease and have the goal of eradication. It is mind boggling to me that this heinous crime continues even today in this 21st century and frustrating to hear stories from so many women of their suffering! If we put our minds we can do anything - that is my belief! First focus of Lets Start W.A.R is to develop the SWear button. This is a wearable technology that will empower the women and children for their own safety.

Lata Sharma

Change happened to me too...when in 1977 I heard, between sobs, from my best friend that her boyfriend had raped her...I did not know how to help and just cried with her ... change happened to me in Dec 16th 2012 when 'Nirbhaya' was raped in Delhi, India. Again...I felt I could only cry with rest of the women in India. Something swept through me as the discussion in the media frustrated me. I was angry. How long will I stand on the sideline and do nothing. I decided it is Now...it is time for CHANGE! It is time for ACTION! I left my corporate career of 13 years to be the change.

I know, we all have stories like this, stories or events that saw a change in us. It is time to drive the change as a collective force, as a community ... as a wave that will give safety to many women. Come join us and lets become a force. I am not even sure where we will go from here. Except that I know I have several dots and with your help I will be able to connect all of them.
